Hi, I'm Sophie! I like to explore new fields and deploy computational methods to solve complex problems.

I'm currently studying mathematics, computer science, and physics at MIT.

I have spent the past three years working at the Structure and Dynamics of Applied Nanomaterials lab at Stony Brook University. I developed a novel mathematical framework incorporating multi-task convolutional auto-encoders that increased the efficiency of X-ray spectrum analysis by 1000x and introduced bi-directional analysis of nanoparticle structure. For this work, I was honored as one of the top 40 American young scientists at the Regeneron Science Talent Search.

Currently, I'm working with the Professor Klavs Jensen's lab at MIT. I'm contributing to ASKCOS, an organic synthesis prediction platform, by developing a new machine learning algorithm for impurity prediction.
